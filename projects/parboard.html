PARBoard

2023-06-01

An ESP32-S2 based micro robotics board. Designed to drive 4 encoded 2A DC motors together with 4 servos. BOM was selected from JLCPCB to allow full assembly, however prototypes were hand-soldered by me. Fully hand-routed in KiCad.
